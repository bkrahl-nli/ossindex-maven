Warn

To prevent build failure configure the fail parameter:

<plugin>
  <groupId>org.sonatype.ossindex.maven</groupId>
  <artifactId>ossindex-maven-plugin</artifactId>
  <configuration>
    <fail>false</fail>
  </configuration>
</plugin>

or via CLI:

mvn ossindex:audit -Dossindex.fail=false
